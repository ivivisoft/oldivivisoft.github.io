Flutter Challenges will attempt to recreate a particular app UI or design in

Getting Started

Before developing a design,identify the components required to make the
screen in any framework.

The app screen has a drawer,a notification and search option,and a list of
articles.

Let’s take a look at the article



At the very top,the article has a text which gives you relevance as to why it’s
there.For example,if it’s based on your reading history or if it’s from a category
you follow.

Below that is the title of the article iteself,which is bold and black as to stand
out.

Below the title is the author of the article,date of publishing,and how long the
article is estimated to take to read.

On the right hand side is the article image and a bookmark button for saving it
for later.
