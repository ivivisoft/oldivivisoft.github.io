Other callbacks from the TextField

The TextField widget also provides other callbacks such as

  1. onEditingCompleted
  2. onSubmitted

These are callbacks called on actions like when the user clicks the “Done”
button on IOS.The onEditingCompleted and onSubmitted trigger on user
tap the Enter key,and the onEditingCompleted trigger first and
onSubmitted trigger on its back.The onSubmitted’s callback function let
the TextField’value to parameter,but onEditingCompleted has no parameter.

Working with focus in TextFields

Having “focus” on a TextField means having a TextField active and any input
form the keyboard will result in data entered in the focused TextField.
